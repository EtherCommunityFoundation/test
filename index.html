Company Profile

Ether Community Foundation (ECF). Headquartered in the United States, it is an organization established by the enthusiastic people who are committed to the innovation and development of the global blockchain industry, research and research, and the venture capital institutions in the blockchain technology field. The Ether Community Foundation is a venture capital fund focused on early investments in blockchain projects.

The Ether Community Foundation offers a variety of professionally reviewed early investment opportunities for foundation members and investment institutions. In addition, the fund is designed to provide many benefits to blockchain entrepreneurs, far beyond financial support.

Public welfare equality, cooperation and win-win, innovation and development. We are at the forefront of technological innovation in the world, relying on blockchain industry veterans and traditional financial industry elites to endorse, committed to popularizing blockchain knowledge, incubating blockchain technology, industry-university research and application, and promoting blockchain technology in global industries. The Ethereum Foundation closely focuses on the innovation and development of blockchain production, research and research, promotes resource sharing and mutual benefit among the members of the Foundation, and promotes the government to formulate and implement the major policies and policies for the development of the blockchain industry, and enhance the group's competitiveness.
